What is Shadow Work?

The Parts of You Running Your Business

🌑

The Shadow

The parts of ourselves we hide, deny, or were taught to suppress. In business, they show up as money blocks, visibility fears, overgiving, and self-sabotage.

💼

Business as Mirror

Your business reflects your inner world. Undercharging = not valuing yourself. Hiding = fear of judgment. Integration transforms both the inner and the outer result.

✦

The Journey

8 weeks. 6 portals. A daily practice moving you from awareness → excavation → integration → aligned action. You will not be the same woman on the other side.
